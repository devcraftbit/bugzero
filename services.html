Our Services

Comprehensive quality assurance and localization designed for flawless gaming experiences.

Core QA Testing

01. Functional Testing

We relentlessly hunt down progression blockers, collision issues, and logic errors. Our rigorous black-box and grey-box methodologies ensure your mechanics work exactly as intended under every possible condition.

  • End-to-end playthroughs & edge case exploration.
  • Exploit discovery and balance assessment.
  • Detailed reproduction steps and severity grading.

02. Performance & Load

Frame drops and infinite loading screens destroy immersion. We push your build to its limits to identify memory leaks, bottleneck assets, and network latency issues before they reach the player.

  • Framerate (FPS) profiling across hardware tiers.
  • Server stress testing and matchmaking stability.
  • Battery drain and thermal testing for handhelds.

By Platform

Specialized teams dedicated to the unique compliance and hardware requirements of every major platform.

PC Gaming

Extensive compatibility testing across a massive matrix of GPUs, CPUs, aspect ratios, and peripheral inputs.

Console

Strict compliance checks for major console ecosystems, ensuring TRC/XR pass rates on the first submission.

Mobile

Optimization for fragmentation. We cover hundreds of devices, checking touch input, OS interrupts, and network shifts.

VR & Cloud

Latency analysis, motion sickness checks, and sensory feedback validation for next-gen immersive titles.

Global Localization

We don't just translate words; we adapt your world. Our localization quality assurance (LQA) ensures your title resonates across diverse audiences without losing its original tone.

Text & UI LQA

String overlap checks, font support, and contextual translation verification.

Audio & VO LQA

Lip-sync validation, subtitle timing, and emotional tone matching.

Cultural Adaptation

Ensuring visual assets, gestures, and references are culturally appropriate and compliant with local regulations.
