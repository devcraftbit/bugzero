Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers regarding our QA process and capabilities.

How long does a standard QA cycle take?

Timelines vary heavily based on the scope and complexity of your title. A standard initial sweep for an indie title might take two weeks, whereas comprehensive QA for a massive open-world RPG occurs continuously alongside development.

Do you test on all major consoles?

Yes. Our test labs are fully equipped with the latest hardware configurations, from modern consoles and VR headsets to a wide spectrum of PC components and mobile devices.

How does the localization process work?

We combine native linguistic experts with experienced gamers. They verify translations in-context to ensure UI elements don't overflow, terminology matches the genre, and cultural references land appropriately.
